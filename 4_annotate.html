Medium

written — composed as text.

transcribed — originally spoken, then written down.

cannot_rate — boilerplate, nav, structural, metadata, fragments, or garbled text.

Turn

monologic — one author → general audience. FAQ/how-to = monologic.

dialogic — participatory: forums, comments, social media, chat, interviews.

Activity

explaining — factual info, concepts, reference material.

recounting — narrating specific events in sequence.

directing — how-to: tutorials, recipes, instructions, troubleshooting.

evaluating — opinions, arguments, praise, complaints, "should"-statements.

promoting — marketing by or for the entity described. Org describing own services = promoting.

creating — poetry, lyrics, verse, scripture/hymns.

Formality

formal — social distance, institutional audience.

informal — reduced distance: colloquial vocab, in-group references, casual stance.
